Privacy Policy

This custom GPT action sends data entered by the user to a Google Apps Script web app in order to append a row to a Google Sheet.

Data processed:

  • text or values submitted by the user
  • request metadata that may be logged by platform providers

How data is used:

  • solely to write submitted values into the connected Google Sheet

Data sharing:

  • data is transmitted to Google Apps Script and stored in Google Sheets
  • no sale of personal data
  • no sharing with third parties except the underlying service providers needed to operate the action

Data retention:

  • submitted data remains in the destination Google Sheet until removed by the sheet owner
